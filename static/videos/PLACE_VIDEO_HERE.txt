=====================================================
  TRC PRODUCTION STUDIOS – Intro Video Setup
=====================================================

Place your intro video file(s) in this folder:

  Required filename:  intro.mp4
  Optional fallback:  intro.webm  (better compression, some browsers)

Video requirements:
  - Format:     MP4 (H.264) + optionally WebM (VP9)
  - Duration:   Recommended 5–20 seconds
  - Resolution: 1920×1080 (16:9) recommended
  - Audio:      Can include audio (video starts muted by default
                to comply with browser autoplay policies)
  - No title cards needed – the site fades in after the video ends

The video will:
  ✓ Play automatically on first-ever visit (localStorage based)
  ✓ Have NO controls, NO pause button, NO progress bar
  ✓ Cover the full screen (object-fit: cover)
  ✓ Dismiss automatically when it ends
  ✓ Have a "SKIP ›" button in the bottom-right corner
  ✓ Show a branded fallback animation if no video file is present

If no video file is found, a branded logo splash runs for 4 seconds.
